About Me

Hi there, My name is Ian Porto, I am currently a Master of Sciences student at York University. My current research involves space situational awareness sensor pointing optimization and some scheduling for them. I am also quite interested in AI-based initial orbit determination.

Given that you probably got here from my ICSSA poster, you should also know that I've done some work on thermo-optical characterization. This work aims to accurately characterize the camera we will deploy on UPMSAT-4. This work is quite important to share, as it provides an overview guide on how to perform this kind of thing for other COTS payloads.
